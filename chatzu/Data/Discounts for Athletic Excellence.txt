A 65% discount is granted to fifteen students who excel in athletics. They are selected according to criteria established by the Council of Deans, upon recommendation from the Deanship of Student Affairs at the university. The following is taken into account: 1. The student's GPA on the General Secondary Education Certificate Examination. 2. The university's need for the sport in which they excel. 3. The student's proficiency in the game and passing the university's level test, provided they are a member or player on national teams, first-division clubs, or education directorate teams in the same year they register at the university.
If a student retakes a subject they passed and fails it after the retake, which of the two marks is counted?
The higher mark is counted.
