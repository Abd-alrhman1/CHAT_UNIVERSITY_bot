Vision

Excellence in providing educational, research, and community services at the local, regional, and international levels.

Mission

Preparing trained and qualified human resources aligned with the needs of the local, regional, and international labor market, and conducting applied scientific research to develop and advance the local community.

Core Values

Freedom of thought and opinion and their independence are the foundation of scientific and social development.
University education is a starting point for unleashing energies, developing talents, and pursuing a career.
Creativity knows no bounds when it finds sponsors.
Scientific research is a means to improve lives.
Quality in everything.
